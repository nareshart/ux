The Problum

Ask any pharmacist why their phone keeps ringing off the hook everyday and 9 times out of 10 they'll tell you it's patients asking where their prescription is. This might not seem like much of a problem, but when you consider the only way they can actually locate it is by navigating away, accessing the NHS Prescription Tracker website and searching there, or if you know for certain it's in your pharmacy then having a look around a room full of prescriptions for it. What's worse is it might not even be in their pharmacy.

This is an awful experience for pharmacy staff but in order to make it as easy as it should be this would take an insane amount of time and work. This project was to ease a little bit of their pain by introducing a new prescription searching integration into a market leading pharmacy product.

The Prescription Tracker is a service provided by the NHS and allows its users to search for a patients prescription no matter what status it's in or pharmacy it's currently being processed by. Currently the product offers a url link out to the service, which is the most clicked item on its dashboard. The dashboard contains a whole host of useful links, information and navigation but this link is a clear favourite with customers.

A simple link out to the service is great, but we could do better, so wanted to look at fully integrating the service so the user never has to leave the product.
